A new landscape for music

Our flagship project and the initial motivator for the Co-founders to form Casl Media. Casl Blue is our attempt at creating a medium for music discovery. We hope bring the underpinning open source mentality of the internet with to the main stream in a historically difficult closed environment.

Casl Media, LLC

We are a thinktank for the Co-founders in the form of an umbrella-esque LLC. We are trying to bring some of our ideas to life. Some of these projects may require substantial resources, e.g. lots of people contributing or lots of hardware, so we formed this Company to help.

Our current team consists of of a mechanical engineer, a full-stack engineer, and database administrator. We are a small but capable team and our motivation spawns from the same principles our projects our built on. Balance of time and funding is the only limitation. We are always looking for to add the right people, typically timing is a significant obstruction.
